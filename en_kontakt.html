      For information about the Zekate House and about the time at which you can visit it, you can contact the Tourist Information Office which is located at the Pass of the Bazaar, in the heart of the historical city, the road that leads to the Castle. There you find free information about:
- Other tourist attractions of Gjirokastra and the region
- Hotels
- Restaurants
- Events and tourist amusement
Also, you can find maps, guides and other publications to help you enjoy your visit to better understand and Gjirokastra, a World Heritage City by UNESCO.

Operating Hours:
9:00 until 17:00, seven days a week from March 1 until November 30, and
9:00 until 15:00, seven days a week from December 1 to February 28.

Tourist Information Office is an initiative of the Organization for the Gjirokastra Conservation and Development (supported by the Packard Humanities Institute) in cooperation with the agency "Old Bazar Travel and Tour Agency".
